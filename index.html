Two Days of Innovation

A curated gathering for the people
building the federated future.

FediCon brings together developers, founders, and advocates of the fediverse and open social web for two focused days of keynotes, deep dives, lightning talks, and live demos — no walled gardens, no shareholders, just the protocols and people behind the fediverse.

Curated Speakers

Hand-picked pioneers and builders sharing the architecture, ideas, and culture driving decentralized social.

Lightning Talks

Fast, high-density sessions where the community shares projects, experiments, and protocol ideas in minutes.

Live Demos

See new apps, clients, and protocol features running in real time, straight from the people who shipped them.
