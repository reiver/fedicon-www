Where To Say for FediCon

If you have never been to Metro Vancouver, figuring out where to stay can be a challenge.

Finding accommodations near the FediCon conference is one option. FediCon is happening at:

UBC Robson Square

800 Robson Street
Vancouver, BC, Canada

And many will choose this option, but — there are other more affordable options.

Metro Vancouver has a mass-transit system known as the SkyTrain. Finding accommodations near any SkyTrain station can be a more affordable option. As, traveling from a SkyTrain station to FediCon (in downtown Vancouver) is relatively quick.

For those who wish to find a more affordable option for accommodations, the map below is a SkyTrain and SeaBus map. After the map are the addresses of each SkyTrain station. Including the address of each SkyTrain station, and an Airbnb link.

Use the address or the Airbnb link to help you find your accommodations outside of downtown Vancouver.
