Where To Say for FediCon

FediCon is happening at:

UBC Robson Square

800 Robson Street
Vancouver, BC, Canada

And, while one could find accommodations near this location, there are other more affordable options.

Metro Vancouver has a mass-transit system known as the SkyTrain.

Finding accommodations near any SkyTrain station can be a more affordable option.

And, to FediCon from any SkyTrain station is relatively quick.
